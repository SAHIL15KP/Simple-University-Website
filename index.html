Main Branches Of SSGI

Engineering & Technology

Hospitality And Management

The institute is affiliated to IKG PTU, Jalandhar and offers educational programs, which develop and enhance skills for job oriented professional hospitality prospects as BBA, B.Com, BCA, B.Sc. MLS, M.Sc. MLS and Hotel Management.

The latest venture of Sri Sukhmani Group of Institutions, aims to become a premier institute in the field of law in the region. SSCL intends to provide students with excellent teaching-learning experience and professional skills through a variety of learning opportunities.
